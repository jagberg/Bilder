# Thermal Insulation

Choosing the right insulation for your walls, ceiling, and roof significantly impacts your home's energy efficiency and comfort.

## Wall & Ceiling Insulation

### Wall Insulation

| R-Rating | Thickness | Cost |
|----------|-----------|------|
| R1.5 | 75–90mm | >$5/m² |
| R2.0 | 75–90mm | >$5/m² |
| R2.2 | 90mm | >$15/m² |
| R2.5 | 90mm | >$11/m² |
| R2.7 | 90mm | >$18/m² |
| R3.0 | 145mm | (ceilings) |
| R4.0 | 140mm | >$20/m² |

Most residential constructions feature 90mm cavities, limiting insulation options. Requesting larger wall frames from builders is uncommon and significantly increases costs. Standard wall ratings typically fall between R1.5 and R2.7, with some applications reaching R4.0.

Material thickness is critical, as it directly constrains the volume of insulation that can be installed.

### Ceiling Insulation

| R-Rating | Thickness | Cost | Notes |
|----------|-----------|------|-------|
| R4.0 | 215mm | >$11/m² | Minimum |
| R5.0 | 240mm | >$16/m² | Standard |
| R6.0 | 260mm | >$20/m² | Typical Upgrade |
| R7.0 | 290mm | >$33/m² | Uncommon |

Small ceiling gaps allow substantial heat transfer. Cross-hatch installation patterns (alternating layers) improve performance.

## Sarking / Wall Wraps

Functions include:

- Draft reduction
- Moisture barrier
- Energy efficiency enhancement (minimal thermal insulation provided)

## Roof Systems

### Anticon

Foil-backed insulation blankets commonly used with Colorbond roofing.

### Roof Sarking

Protective layer providing:

- Mould prevention from storm-driven rain infiltration
- Dust containment
- Enhanced thermal performance via specialised products
- Moisture management options
- Bushfire Attack Level (BAL) compliance capabilities
