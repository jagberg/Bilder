# Great Sources of Information for Building a New Home

## Social Media

### Facebook

#### Builder Groups

These communities feature customers sharing build progress, asking questions about inclusions, posting photos, and providing feedback on builders. Membership isn't restricted to customers of specific companies.

Available groups include:

- Building with Vogue Homes
- Building with Fowler Homes in NSW
- Building with Fairmont Homes
- Wisdom Homes Customers Sydney
- Building with G.J. Gardner

#### General Groups

Broader discussion spaces covering multiple topics:

- **My Efficient Electric Home (MEEH)** — Focuses on energy efficiency, covering hot water systems, solar, windows, insulation and many other topics
- **Building New Homes in Sydney and NSW** — Addresses builder-specific and broader construction subjects
- **Building New Australian Homes** — Nationwide coverage with comprehensive building discussions

### Instagram

Personal build accounts and trade/designer pages provide progress documentation and design inspiration. Notable examples span kitchen joinery, staircases, landscaping, concrete work, stonework, and interior design.

## Websites

- **Product Review** — Builder reviews available, though community feedback on Facebook groups may offer more authentic perspectives
- **Homeone** — Builder information and community forums for construction discussions
- **Pinterest** — Inspiration and design ideas
