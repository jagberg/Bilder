# Pools

Adding a pool to your new home build is a significant investment. The main material choices are fibreglass and concrete.

## Material Options

### Fibreglass

Fibreglass pools are pre-formed shells installed into an excavated area.

**Advantages:**
- Faster installation
- Smoother surface
- Lower ongoing maintenance costs
- Cost: $50,000–$75,000

### Concrete

Concrete pools are built on-site and can be customised to any shape or size.

**Advantages:**
- Fully customisable shape and size
- More durable for complex designs
- Cost: $60,000–$100,000+

## Location Relative to the House

Pool placement requires careful consideration of:

- Setback requirements from property boundaries and structures
- Access for construction equipment
- Impact on landscaping and drainage
- Proximity to the house for safety and convenience
