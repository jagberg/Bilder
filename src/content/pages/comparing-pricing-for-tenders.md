# Comparing Pricing of Tenders Against Other Builders

It's important to do a like-for-like comparison as many quotes will leave out items that you will need. Use a detailed cost breakdown table to compare effectively.

## Key Pricing Categories

### Initial Costs

- Home base price
- Demolition (approximately $34,000 as of 2022)
- Architect fees
- Landscape planning

### Site-Specific Costs

Various location-dependent expenses include:

- Traffic control plans
- Slab upgrades (H1 slabs around $4,450)
- Rock and soil removal (each ~$5,000)
- Concrete pump services (~$1,290)
- Hydraulic/stormwater management (~$28,000)
- Marine condition treatments (~$5,000)
- Acoustic treatments (potentially $10,000+)

### Building Components

**Windows & Doors:**

- Double glazing: ~$25,000
- Skylights: ~$2,475
- Door upgrades: $1,000–$1,200

**Internal Finishes:**

- Paint and ceilings: $2,000–$3,000
- Soundproofing: $3,500–$4,500
- Staircase upgrades: ~$22,000

**Kitchen & Bathrooms:**

- Kitchen allowance: $25,000
- Benchtops: $3,500
- Bathroom tiles: $11,000

## Important Considerations

Many builders include certain items in base prices while others charge separately. Promotional packages may also affect final pricing significantly.

Always itemise every cost component and confirm what's included and excluded before making a final comparison.
