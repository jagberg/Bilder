# Budget

When budgeting for a new home build, it's important to understand all the cost components beyond the base build price.

## Base Price

The base price is the starting cost for building on a level block without design modifications.

## Site Costs ($20,000–$140,000+)

Site-specific costs can vary enormously depending on your land and location.

### Sloping Land Costs

**Drop Edge Beams (retaining walls):** $0–$40,000+

**Split-level design:** $0–$40,000+

**Hydraulics/stormwater management:** $10,000–$30,000

Options include onsite detention tanks, charged systems, silt arrestor pits, and spreader systems. Easements may be required for water flow across neighbours' properties.

### Additional Site Expenses

- Rock removal: $5,000–$40,000
- Soil import/export costs
- Traffic management/control: $5,000–$10,000
- Extra piering (for non-fixed price contracts)

## Inclusions

Common builder inclusions to compare across tenders:

- 2740mm ceilings (double storey)
- Polyurethane kitchens
- 40mm stone benchtops
- 600×600mm tiles or laminate

## Owner Costs

### Demolition ($22,000–$40,000+)

- CDC approval: $880–$1,000
- Gas abolishment: $1,141
- Electricity disconnection: ~$300

### Council Contributions

- Approximately 1% of build cost for single homes
- Sections 7.11/7.12 contributions ($8,400–$20,000 for other property types)
- Varies by council; some exempt certain building types

### Infrastructure

- Driveway (coloured concrete): $9,000–$13,000
- Crossover replacement: $4,000–$5,000

### OHS Requirements

- Tiger tails (overhead wire protection): $2,000
- Pool covers/hard covers: $2,400–$4,000

### Optional Features

- Pools: $50,000–$75,000 (fibreglass) to $60,000–$100,000+ (concrete)
- Solar panels: $5,000–$25,000
- Tesla battery: $16,000

### Professional Services

- Independent building inspection: $800–$4,000
- Arborist reports: ~$1,500
- Acoustic reports: $1,650–$3,800
- BAL (bushfire) reports: cost varies
- Landscape designer: $800+

### Post-Build Work

- Landscaping: $15,000–$25,000+ (for 400–500 sqm blocks)
- Front fencing: $12,000+
- Retaining walls: cost varies by design
- Steps/outdoor features: variable costs
