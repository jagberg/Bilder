# Helpful Home Visualisation Apps

These digital tools can help you visualise your new home during the planning and design phases.

## Check What You Can Build

**Canibuild**

A paid service enabling users to verify buildable specifications on their site, overlay plans for DA or CDC compliance checks. Useful for reviewing council setbacks, FSR, and contours.

**Council and State Sites**

- Local council websites (e.g., Georges River Council) provide contour surveys, bushfire zones, noise mapping, and stormwater infrastructure details
- NSW Spatial Portal offers topographic maps and additional geographic data

## 2D Floorplans

**MagicPlan** — Mobile app and browser-based tool for tracing floorplan PDFs and experimenting with spatial layouts

**FloorplanCreator** — Platform for adjusting room dimensions and repositioning spaces within designs

## 3D Floorplans

| Software | Cost | Complexity | Features |
|----------|------|-----------|----------|
| Floorplanner.com | Free/Paid | Moderate | PDF tracing, wall adjustment, workarounds for complex elements |
| SketchUp | Free/Paid | Advanced | Detailed 3D visualisation with material imports |
| ArchiCAD | Trial/Paid | Comprehensive | Professional-grade tool producing 2D plans, elevations, 3D views, and schedules |
| Revit | Trial/Paid | Comprehensive | Architect-standard software with customisable elements |

## Specialised Design Tools

**Exterior Colours/Bricks:**

- Austral iVisualise
- Plantation Homes Visualiser (includes interior styling)

**Interior Design:**

- HomeByMe (room styling app)

**Kitchen Design:**

- ArchiCAD and Home Designer by Chief Architect (both professional-level with steeper learning curves)

**Bathrooms:**

- Reece Imagin3D (3D bathroom layouts with fixtures)
- Beaumont Tiles Visualiser
