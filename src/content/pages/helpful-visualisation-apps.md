# Helpful Home Visualisation Apps

## Check what you can build

#### canibuild

[Canibuild](https://canibuild.com/en-au/) – is a paid service that you can use to check what you can build on your site. You can also overlay a plan onto it to see how it complies with a DA or CDC application.

Great for

- Checking council setbacks, FSR, contours

#### Council and State Sites

- Council websites like [Georges River Council](https://intramaps.georgesriver.nsw.gov.au/) – contour surveys (hydraulics), bushfire zones, noise zones (ANEF), stormwater pits and pipes, etc.
- [NSW Spatial Portal](https://portal.spatial.nsw.gov.au/portal/apps/webappviewer/index.html) – Contours, topographic maps, and lots of other details.

## 2D Floorplans

[magicplan](https://www.magicplan.app/) – Best used through their mobile app but you can use on a browser too. You can quickly trace a pdf of a floorplan and then play around with the layout.

[flooorplancreator](https://floorplancreator.net/) – Good for moving rooms around once they've been created. You can easily create the dimensions you like and then move rooms/spaces around. You can use a browser or an app.

## Exterior Colours and Bricks

These sites include homes with different storeys and styles. You can customise the exterior with the different bricks that are available as well as the different colours for things like the roof, gutters, render, windows, etc.

[Austral iVisualise](https://australbricks.com.au/ivisualise/)

[Plantation Homes Visualiser](https://plantationhomes.com.au/home-designs/inspiration/visualiser) – Includes internal styling and colours too

## Interior Design

[floorplanner.com](https://floorplanner.com/) – Free but you can pay for extras like the ability to have multiple stories in one design. You can design it so that each floor is side by side for visualisation though.

[Plantation Homes Visualiser](https://plantationhomes.com.au/home-designs/inspiration/visualiser) – Includes internal and external styling and colours.

[HomeByMe](https://home.by.me/en/) – App to style rooms

## Bathrooms

[Reece Imagin3D](https://imagin3d.reece.com.au/3d-planner/plan/new/) – Gives you the ability to create a 3D bathroom with the fixtures available from Reece. The tiles are a bit limited but it can give you a good idea of what the layout feels like.

[Beaumont Tiles Visualiser](https://legacy.tile.com.au/)
