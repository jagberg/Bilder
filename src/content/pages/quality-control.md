# Quality Control and Private Inspections for a New Build Home

Quality control during construction involves two main approaches: building surveyors and independent building inspectors.

## Building Surveyor

A building surveyor differs from a building inspector. Under building codes, homeowners may choose their own surveyor to sign off on various construction phases. However, volume builders typically offer to select one on the owner's behalf, usually from companies they regularly work with.

The surveyor technically represents the homeowner but often provides limited direct interaction or information sharing with the owner.

## Independent Building Inspector

While builders employ site supervisors, these professionals often manage numerous properties simultaneously — sometimes up to 15 properties. This stretched capacity can result in missed critical construction elements leading to defects.

Hiring an independent inspector at various construction stages helps ensure quality meets expectations. While inspectors cannot force builders to address issues, documented defects that violate building standards are difficult to dispute.

## Common Inspection Stages

### Pre-Slab Inspection

Examines slab dimensions, vapour/termite barriers, concrete beam dimensions, formwork levelness, reinforcement placement, and plumbing positioning.

### Frame Inspection

Verifies room dimensions, window/door positions, framing squareness, correct sizing and bracing, and floor bounce levels.

### Pre-Lining Inspection

Checks plumbing/electrical placement, previous defect corrections, framing integrity, roof coverings, and brick tie installation.

### Lock-Up/Waterproofing

Assesses plaster installation, finishing standards, carpentry quality, and wet area waterproofing compliance.

### Practical Completion Inspection (PCI)

Reviews all interior and exterior finishes, fixtures, bathroom/kitchen functionality, floor coverings, paint quality, and window conditions.

### End of Defect Liability Period Inspection

Confirms earlier identified issues have been properly addressed before warranty coverage expires.
